The Pink Porsche Taycan: An Interior as Beautiful as The Exterior

The color we’re talking about on the pink Porsche Taycan is actually called Frozen Berry Metallic shade.

It’s a pinkish, steely rose paint color that gives the Porsche Taycan a rather feminine but somehow edgy look. And it’s very popular.

And it’s a nice break from most of the car colors on the road: white, brown, blue, and so forth
